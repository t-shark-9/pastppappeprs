      polluting the landscape.

      Salin and Michael of the Treak Community Centre near Siem Reap have developed an innovative
      way of dealing with the issue of plastic bag waste.

      fire. This shrinks the bags and makes them stiffer. The bags are then shredded into small chunks
      with scissors, see Figure 10. The shredded plastic is then used as a substitute for aggregate in
      concrete which is mixed by hand, see Figure 11. This concrete is then moulded into bricks, see
      Figure 12 and Figure 13, that can be used for construction.
