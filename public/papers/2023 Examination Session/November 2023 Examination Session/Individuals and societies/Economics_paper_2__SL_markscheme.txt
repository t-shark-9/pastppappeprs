‚Ä¢ improved public transport will improve the environment while also increasing growth

Ways in which growth does conflict with environmental sustainability:
‚Ä¢ increased GDP/capita leads to more spending on cars and electricity (Table 1;
  Text C, paragraph 1)

‚Ä¢ budgetary constraints in the future mean less government expenditure available for

  paragraph 5)
