1.    Compare and contrast the contribution of Tewodros II and Yohannis IV to the reunification of Ethiopia.


2.    Analyse the contribution of either Mirambo to the rise of Unyamwezi or of Mkwawa to the rise of
      the Hehe state.


3.    Compare and contrast the achievements of Nana and JaJa as rulers of Niger Delta trading states.


4.    To what extent did the organization of the Zulu kingdom under Shaka contribute to the Mfecane?


5.    “The key to understanding the sudden scramble for Africa is to be found in events in Egypt between
      1879 and 1882.” To what extent do you agree with this statement as an explanation of the European
      annexation of Africa?


6.    To what extent was the European partition of Africa determined by economic and humanitarian
      factors?


7.    Analyse the causes and results for both Africans and the colonial power of the Ndebele–Shona rising
      (1896–1897).


8.    Analyse the reasons for, and the results of, Kabaka Mwanga’s responses to the gradual loss of
      Buganda’s independence.


9.    “Cetshwayo was not to blame for the conquest and destruction of the Zulu kingdom.” To what extent
      do you agree with this statement?


10.   Compare and contrast the reasons for British intervention in Asante and French intervention in the
      Mandinka Empire in the nineteenth century.


11.   “The Boers lost the war but won the peace.” To what extent do you agree with this statement about
      the results of the South African War (1899–1902)?


12.   Assess the relative importance of internal and external factors contributing to the end of apartheid.


13.   “The people of Tanganyika benefited more from British rule than from German rule.” To what extent
      do you agree with this statement?

14.   Assess the economic development of the Gold Coast under colonial rule to 1957.


15.   Analyse the reasons for, and the extent of, the spread of Islam in either West or East Africa in the
      nineteenth century.


16.   For what reasons, and to what extent, did African art and culture change in the colonial period?
      Answer with specific reference to either West or Eastern and Central Africa.


17.   For what reasons did the Central African Federation collapse and in what ways did the collapse
      contribute to the achievement of independence in Malawi?


18.   Analyse the nature of the Mau Mau rising and its contribution to the achievement of independence
      in Kenya.


19.   To what extent did regional rivalry delay the achievement of independence in Nigeria?


20.   Compare and contrast the factors that helped and hindered the achievement of independence in Angola
      and Namibia.


21.   Define neocolonialism and analyse its impact on any two countries in Africa since independence.


22.   With reference to any two independent African countries, analyse the reasons for, and the results up
      until 2000, of the return to multiparty democracy.


23.   To what extent did the Abyssinian Crisis (1935–1936) destroy the credibility of the League of Nations?


24.   With reference to any two countries, assess the impact of the Cold War on Africa.
