These notes to examiners are intended only as guidelines to assist marking. They are not offered as
an exhaustive and fixed set of responses or approaches to which all answers must rigidly adhere.
Good ideas or angles not offered here should be acknowledged and rewarded as appropriate.
Similarly, answers which do not include all the ideas or approaches suggested here should not be so
heavily penalized as to distort appreciation of individuality.

These questions invite the candidates to consider the Cultural Option “Language and culture”.

1.   A barely satisfactory answer will offer a general discussion about politically
     sensitive language. One or two examples will be given.

     A better answer will draw on appropriate examples in making its argument and will have a
     coherent structure.

     support those arguments with examples that are pertinent, precise, and varied. The essays will

These questions invite the candidates to consider a Literary Option.

8.    A barely satisfactory answer will offer a general discussion of the role of passion in at least
      two works studied. It will use at least one example from each text.

      A better answer will draw on appropriate evidence to make an argument about the role of
      passion in at least two works studied. It will have a coherent structure.

      The best answers will draw on pertinent and varied examples to make a persuasive argument
      about the role of passion in at least two works studied. It will discuss theme in some detail
      and will be well organized.


9.    A barely satisfactory answer will offer a general discussion of the way characters
      “come of age” in two works studied. It will use at least one supporting example of
      the psychological, moral, or social shaping of a character from each text.

      A better answer will draw on appropriate evidence to offer a reasonable discussion of how
      characters “come of age” in at least two works studied. The essay will demonstrate a good
      understanding of the texts and will have a coherent structure.

      The best answers will use a range of precise and pertinent examples to discuss how characters
      come of age in at least two works studied. They will be well organized and persuasive.


10.   A barely satisfactory answer will offer a general discussion of the role of religion in at least
      two works studied. It will use at least one example from each text.

      A better answer will draw on appropriate evidence to make an argument about the role that
      religion plays in at least two works studied. It will address the importance of religion to the
      works’ central ideas and themes and will have a coherent structure.

      The best answers will draw on pertinent and varied examples to make a persuasive argument
      about the role of religion and its importance in at least two works studied. It will discuss
      theme in some detail and will be well organized.
