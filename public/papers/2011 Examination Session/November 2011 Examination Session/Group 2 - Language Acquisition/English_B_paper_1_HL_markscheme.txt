TEXT D — BACK TO SLEEP, BACK TO SCHOOL
Communicative purpose: Guidelines/factual description
A.   Cultural interaction:
     The aim of the task is to write an e-mail to the candidate’s grandmother, advising her how to
     improve her mental and physical abilities. The candidate must clearly express the view that it
     is possible to improve mental and physical health by adhering to the advice given.
     Register and style: the register may be informal and the style may be lively as long as it is
     respectful and caring.
     Language accuracy is not formally assessed here and should be considered only where
     mistakes obscure meaning.
     For Criterion A, see Page 44 of Language B guide.
B.   Message:
     A good response will address several positive points raised in the original text but should not
     include negative aspects unless it is to refute them (see detail G). The descriptors regarding
     copying from the text should be carefully adhered to.
     Verbatim copying of short sections of the text does NOT constitute “undue copying”;
     the copying of any section (whether it be long or short) without using it appropriately and
     incorporating it sensibly into the response is what constitutes undue copying.
     The candidate should include some of the following details from the text:
     A.   Go back to school and help out in class / volunteer (for 15 hours a week) in classrooms.
     B.   Associate with other people / stay socially connected.
     C.   Be physically active / being with children makes you physically active.
     D.   Move your exercise bike in front of the TV.
     E.   Stand up and walk whilst on the phone.
     F.   Do static exercises / consult your doctor to find out what (static) exercises will suit you.
     G.   Be careful not to strain any muscles (paragraph 6) / do not tire yourself (paragraph 4) /
          be careful when dealing with children; you do not want to risk catching a childhood
          disease that any of the children may have (paragraph 5).
     H.   Get a good night’s sleep – seven or eight hours should be sufficient.

CRITERION B:

     To achieve level 5 (adequate understanding), at least four details should be included.
     To achieve level 9 (very good understanding), at least seven details should be included.
