Answer one question. Refer closely to the novel you have selected to study, a clean copy of which may be
brought into the examination.

1.    Select a passage from a novel you have chosen to study in which jealousy is the dominant action.
      Discuss how you would stage this scene for an audience, paying particular attention to the contributions
      made by the actors.


2.    Select a passage from a novel you have chosen to study where a character is the witness to an
      important event. How would you stage this event to heighten its impact on an audience?


3.    Select a passage from a novel you have chosen to study which contains a key dialogue between two
      characters. Show how you would stage this dialogue for an audience, paying particular attention to
      the movements of the actors and what they say.
