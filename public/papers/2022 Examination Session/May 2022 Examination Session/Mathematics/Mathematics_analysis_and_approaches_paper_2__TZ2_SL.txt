8.   [Maximum mark: 12]

     A scientist conducted a nine-week experiment on two plants, A and B , of the same species.
     He wanted to determine the effect of using a new plant fertilizer. Plant A was given fertilizer
     regularly, while Plant B was not.

     The scientist found that the height of Plant A , hA cm , at time t weeks can be modelled by the
     function hA (t ) = sin (2t + 6) + 9t + 27 , where 0 ≤ t ≤ 9 .

     The scientist found that the height of Plant B , hB cm , at time t weeks can be modelled by the
     function hB (t ) = 8t + 32 , where 0 ≤ t ≤ 9 .

     (a)   Use the scientist’s models to find the initial height of

           (i)    Plant B ;

           (ii)   Plant A correct to three significant figures.                                         [3]

     (b)   Find the values of t when hA (t ) =  hB (t ) .                                               [3]

     (c)   For 0 ≤ t ≤ 9 , find the total amount of time when the rate of growth of Plant B was
           greater than the rate of growth of Plant A .[6]
