Section 1: Indigenous societies and cultures in the Americas (c750–1500)

1.    Discuss the role of tribute in two indigenous societies or cultures in pre-Columbian Americas.


2.    Discuss the role of religion in two indigenous societies or cultures in pre-Columbian Americas.



Section 2: European explorations and conquests in the Americas (c1492–c1600)

3.    Compare and contrast French and British exploration and conquest in the Americas.


4.    Evaluate the impact of Spanish exploration and conquest on the indigenous populations in
      the Americas.



Section 3: Colonial government in the New World (1500–1800)

5.    Evaluate the impact of European mercantilist policies on colonial American economies.


6.    “Resistance to colonial authority was mainly motivated by political concerns.” Discuss.



Section 4: Religion in the New World (1500–1800)

7.    “British North American colonies mostly practiced religious tolerance.” To what extent do you
      agree with this statement?


8.    “The impact of the Great Awakening c1720–c1760 was more social than political.” Discuss.



Section 5: Slavery and the New World (1500–1800)

9.    “Economic factors were the main reason for the introduction of slavery in the New World.” Discuss.


10.   To what extent was opposition to the slave trade and slavery in British America successful?

Section 6: Independence movements (1763–1830)

11.   Examine the importance of foreign intervention on the success of independence movements
      in the Americas.


12.   Evaluate the military contributions of two of the following leaders to the process of
      independence: Washington, Bolivar or San Martin.



Section 7: Nation-building and challenges (c1780–c1870)

13.   “The Articles of Confederation were largely successful in resolving the challenges that came
      with independence.” Discuss.


14.   “The 1837 rebellions in Canada were caused more by political than economic factors.” Discuss.



Section 8: US Civil War: causes, course and effects (1840–1877)

15.   To what extent was westward expansion the main cause of the Civil War?


16.   “Reconstruction was an economic failure, but a political success by 1877.” Discuss.



Section 9: The development of modern nations (1865–1929)

17.   “Immigration had a largely positive impact on the development of nations.” Discuss with
      reference to two countries in the Americas.


18.   Evaluate the contributions to the search for civil rights of two of the following leaders:
      Booker T Washington, W E B Du Bois or Marcus Garvey.



Section 10: Emergence of the Americas in global affairs (1880–1929)

19.   “Political factors were the main cause of United States’ expansionist foreign policy.” Discuss.


20.   Examine the impact of the First World War on the foreign policy of any two countries of the
      Americas.
