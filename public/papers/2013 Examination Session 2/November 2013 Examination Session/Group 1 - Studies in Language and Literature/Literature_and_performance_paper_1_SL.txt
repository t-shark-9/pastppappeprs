Answer one question. Refer closely to the novel you have selected to study, a clean copy of which may be
brought into the examination.

1.    Select a passage from the novel you have chosen to study in which a character appears alone in a
      particular setting. Discuss how you would stage this passage for an audience in order to convey
      its importance.


2.    Choose a passage from the novel you have chosen to study and show how, through staging and action,
      a dominant theme contained in it might be conveyed dramatically to an audience.


3.    Select a passage from the novel you have chosen to study which deals with human love. Discuss how
      you would stage this in order to convey to the audience the drama of that love.
