                                   Figure 5: “Building back better”




Dominica is focusing on a “Building back better” programme of recovery after Hurricane Maria to:

y   become the world’s first climate-resilient nation
y   conduct disaster risk assessment before reconstruction
y   build hurricane-proof buildings
y   build using local skills and resources
y   focus on community participation in reconstruction
y   plant fast-growing crops, such as lettuce and Chinese cabbage
y   focus on root crops, such as sweet potatoes and ginger
y   plant heat-tolerant varieties of foods
y   keep farm animals in air-conditioned buildings.
