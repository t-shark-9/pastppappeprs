Write an essay on one of the following. You must base your answer on the cultural options or literary
options you have studied in class. References to other subjects or literary works are permissible but should
not form the main body of your answer.

Language and culture

1.    How have other languages influenced English and with what effect? Discuss with reference to your
      own experiences and study of the option.


2.    Language is not just a means of communication, it is more a matter of national identity. Comment on
      this statement using specific examples and illustrations from your study of this option.



Media and culture

3.    For better or for worse we live in a “world” created and maintained by magazines, film, Internet and
      advertising companies. Discuss this statement with clear reference to your study of this option.


4.    In what ways has the powerful reach of the mass media both broadened and limited our world view
      of different nations, cultures and people?



Future issues

5.    Will technology be a hindrance or a benefit to our future? Why? Discuss using specific examples
      from your study of this option.



Global issues

6.    What part – if any – might national or international education play in addressing the global issues you
      have studied?



Social issues

7.    Based on your study of this option, which social groups seem to be most at risk and what can be done
      to assure their rights and well-being?
