Write an essay on one of the following. You must base your answer on the cultural options or literary
options you have studied in class. References to other subjects or literary works are permissible but should
not form the main body of your answer.

Language and culture

1.    Is it possible to learn a new language without becoming familiar with a new culture? Discuss with
      reference to your study of this option.


2.    Do you agree that it is important to safeguard the world’s languages? Discuss with reference to your
      study of this option.



Media and culture

3.    Does the mass media promote positive social values and attitudes in young children and teenagers?
      Discuss with reference to your study of this option and to at least two media sources such as magazines,
      Internet, film, computer games and television.


4.    In your opinion which media sources are most likely to communicate information that is reliable
      and accurate? In your answer refer to at least two sources of media studied in this option.



Future issues

5.    Progress always leads to positive changes. With reference to your study of future issues, discuss
      whether you agree or disagree with this statement.



Global issues

6.    “Almost all global problems could be solved within a year if our priorities were different.”
      With reference to your study of this option, what are our priorities and why do they prevent certain
      global issues from being resolved?



Social issues

7.    Do men and women play similar roles in resolving certain problems in society? Discuss with reference
      to your study of this option.
