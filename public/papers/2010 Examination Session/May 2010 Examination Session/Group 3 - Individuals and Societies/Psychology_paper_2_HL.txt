Comparative Psychology

1.    Discuss inter-species and intra-species communication strategies (e.g. visual, auditory,
      olfactory).                                                                              [20 marks]


2.    Using examples from research studies and/or findings, explain how ethical considerations
      may affect research in comparative psychology.                                           [20 marks]


3.    To what extent do courtship and parenting strategies of non-human animals impact on
      their survival?                                                                     [20 marks]


Cultural Psychology

4.    (a)   Define the term “culture”.                                                         [4 marks]

      (b)   Discuss how findings from research studies in cultural psychology have
            been applied.                                                          [16 marks]


5.    Describe and evaluate two empirical studies from cultural psychology.                   [20 marks]


6.    Discuss cultural variations in identity.                                                [20 marks]

The Psychology of Dysfunctional Behaviour

Health Psychology

10.   Explain how gender considerations may affect the interpretation of behaviour related to
      health psychology.                                                                      [20 marks]


11.   Using empirical studies, describe and evaluate two different research methods

12.   Discuss how research findings about psychological aspects of stress have been applied. [20 marks]

15.   Describe and evaluate two empirical studies related to socialization in lifespan psychology.   [20 marks]
