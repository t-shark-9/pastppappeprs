1.    Compare and contrast the achievements of Kabaka Mutesa of Buganda and Mirambo of Unyamwezi
      as rulers.


2.    Assess the success of Lewanika as a nation builder.


3.    “Mosheshwe was a more successful ruler than Shaku Zulu.” To what extent do you agree with
      this statement?


4.    Assess the relative importance of political, social and economic factors as causes of the rise of the
      Mandinka Empire.


5.    To what extent did explorers and traders contribute to the European partition of Africa?


6.    To what extent did the activities of King Leopold II of Belgium and de Brazza in the Congo region
      accelerate the European annexation of Africa?


7.    For what reasons did Menelik II succeed in maintaining Ethiopia’s independence?


8.    Compare and contrast the causes and results of the Maji Maji Rising of 1905 and John Chilembwe’s
      rising of 1915.


9.    Analyse the reasons for, and the results of, the Anglo–Asante confrontations up until 1901.


10.   For what reasons, and with what results, did the Herero resist German rule in Namibia?


11.   Analyse the economic, social and political consequences of the discovery of diamonds and gold
      in South Africa.


12.   Assess the importance of De Klerk in the history of South Africa.


13.   Compare and contrast the colonial administrative systems of the British in Tanganyika and the
      Portuguese in Mozambique.


14.   “French rule benefited the Senegalese more than British rule benefited the people of Ghana (the Gold
      Coast).” To what extent do you agree with this statement?

15.   Why did some Africans join the Independent Church Movement while others joined or remained
      members of missionary Christian churches?


16.   Analyse the obstacles to the suppression of the slave trade in West or East Africa. How was the trade
