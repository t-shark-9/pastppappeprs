Write an essay on one of the following. You must base your answer on the cultural options or literary
options you have studied in class. References to other subjects or literary works are permissible but should
not form the main body of your answer.

Language and culture

1.    With reference to your study of this option consider the idea that a language evolves to express the
      culture and experience of its users.


2.    It has been said that a different language gives one a different vision of life. Discuss this idea with
      reference to your study of this option.



Media and culture

3.    Mass media do not exist to inform or entertain. They exist to make money. Discuss.


4.    With reference to your study of this option, consider the idea that advertising promotes discontent.



Future issues

5.    Discuss the positive and negative effects that one area of technology will have in future years.



Global issues

6.    It is often said that the world is getting smaller. Consider how this idea is relevant to one or two
      global issues studied.



Social issues

7.    Social problems are inevitably linked, so one cannot be solved without addressing the other.
      Discuss with reference to two social issues studied.

Literary questions
