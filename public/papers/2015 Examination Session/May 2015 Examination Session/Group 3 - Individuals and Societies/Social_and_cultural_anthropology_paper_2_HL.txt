1.    Discuss the impact of globalization on one of the following:

      • systems of production
      • gender relations
      • religion.


2.    Examine the relationship between political organization and either leadership or egalitarianism.


3.    To what extent is agency constrained or enabled by social class?


4.    Examine the importance of ideology in maintaining kinship systems.


5.    Discuss power and resistance in one society or social group.


6.    Examine the role of economic organization in one of the following:

      • migration
      • relationships with the environment
      • tourism.


7.    Discuss what part modern technologies play in either indigenous movements or social
      movements.


8.    Examine how either modernity or changes in consumption practices have shaped one of the
      following:

      • identity
      • sexuality
      • ethnicity.


9.    To what extent do transformations of exchange systems alter either marriage or labour practices?

      • human rights
