Write an essay on one of the following. You must base your answer on the cultural options or literary
options you have studied in class. References to other subjects or literary works are permissible but should
not form the main body of your answer.

Language and culture

1.    In what ways is our use of language changing? Discuss, using examples from the option studied.


2.    Today’s multicultural, multilingual countries must develop new ways of sharing a sense of
      national identity. Discuss, using specific examples from the option studied.



Media and culture

3.    We have become far too dependent on the mass media. Discuss with reference to your study of
      this option.


4.    Using examples from two different types of media, analyse some of the methods used by each to
      appeal to particular audiences.



Future issues

5.    What problem must the human race solve before the end of this century? Explain why you have
      chosen this problem and what immediate steps should be taken to solve it.



Global issues

6.    “Think globally, act locally” is a slogan that is often heard. Using specific examples from your study
      of the option, explain what the slogan means and whether you agree with it.



Social issues

7.    Social problems can eventually make a society stronger. Do you agree? Explain, using examples
      from your study of the option.

Literary questions

8.    How do weather or climate play an important role in at least two literary works you have studied?


9.    Many works of literature include characters who are opposites of each other. Analyse contrasting
      characters in at least two works you have studied. Why are these contrasts important to each work?


10.   Sometimes an unhappy ending is the most effective ending to a work of literature. Choose at least two
      endings that might be considered unhappy. Discuss whether they are effective and why.
