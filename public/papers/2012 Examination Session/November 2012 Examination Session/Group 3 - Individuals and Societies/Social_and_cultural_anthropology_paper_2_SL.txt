Answer two questions. Candidates must use specific and clearly identified ethnographic illustrations.
Each question is worth [19 marks]. Up to an additional [6 marks] are awarded across both answers.

1.    Discuss the relationship between the environment and one of the following:

      •     political organization
      •     economic organization
      •     colonialism
      •     religion.


2.    Discuss the relationship between kinship and either gender or inequality.


3.    Examine how either colonialism or globalization has shaped one of the following:

      •     human suffering
      •     group identity
      •     resistance
      •     commodification.


4.    Contrast socialization processes in two societies.


5.    Discuss the role of agency in economic organization.


6.    Examine the importance of ritual in political organization.


7.    Examine the relationship between power and either migration or tourism.


8.    Examine the relationship between gender and division of labour.


9.    Discuss how modernity has affected local exchange systems.


10.   Discuss how contact between different societies can result in new forms of one of the following:

      •     inequality
      •     social movements
      •     systems of production and consumption
      •     belief systems.
