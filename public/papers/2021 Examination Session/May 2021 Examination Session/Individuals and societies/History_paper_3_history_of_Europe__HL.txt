Section 1: Monarchies in England and France (1066–1223)

1.    “Henry II’s campaigns in Europe were defensive rather than aggressive.” To what extent do
      you agree with this statement?


2.    Compare and contrast the changing nature of government in France and England.



Section 2: Muslims and Jews in medieval Europe (1095–1492)

3.    “The conflict between Christians and Muslims led to the loss of cultural and intellectual
      diversity.” Discuss.


4.    Evaluate the role of the Black Death in the persecution of Jews in medieval Europe.



Section 3: Late medieval political crises (1300–1487)

5.    “Weak government was the main cause of the deposition of Edward II.” To what extent do
      you agree with this statement?


6.    “Louis XI successfully dealt with the challenges to his power.” To what extent do you agree
      with this statement?



Section 4: The Renaissance (c1400–1600)

7.    Evaluate the contribution of social and political factors to the development of the
      Renaissance in Florence.


8.    Evaluate the role of papal patronage in promoting the arts.



Section 5: The Age of Exploration and its impact (1400–1550)

9.    Examine the consequences of Portuguese exploration of the west coast of Africa for
      European states.


10.   “The impact of the ‘Columbian Exchange’ on Europe was positive.” To what extent do you
      agree with this statement?
