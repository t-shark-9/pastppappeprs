Answer one question. Refer closely to the works of at least two poets you have studied.

1.   How have at least two poets you have studied dealt with the question of morality in their poetry?


2.   Referring to the work of at least two poets, discuss how they have explored the concepts of
     existence and/or nothingness through imagery.


3.   In the work of at least two poets you have studied, analyse how they have used concepts to
     create a world of illusion.


4.   Referring closely to the work of at least two poets you have studied, discuss the ways they have
     used symbolism and metaphor where betrayal is the subject of the text.


5.   In poetry, the values expressed are complemented by the form and structure of the poem.
     In the work of at least two poets you have studied, compare how they have used form to create
     meaning.


6.   In the work of at least two poets you have studied, analyse how they have used language,
     structure, rhythm and tone to create suspense and intrigue for the reader.
